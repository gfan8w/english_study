# accountable/account for

hold someone accountable

**英/ əˈkaʊntəbl /美/ əˈkaʊntəbl /**

- *adj.***负有责任的，应作解释的；可理解的**

中文中「追究责任」翻译成英文是 hold someone accountable.

I need someone accountable for this task.

简而言之,，accountable就是，如果搞砸了，谁要承担责任的意思。

One thing with democracy is its political accountability.

This involves an error or a condition that they haven’t accounted for.

account for:  对……负责，对……负有责任：对某事物负有责任或义务。
